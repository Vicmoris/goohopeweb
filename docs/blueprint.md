# **App Name**: GoodHope Hub

## Core Features:

- School Information Display: Display comprehensive information about GoodHope International School, including its history, mission, and values.
- Admissions Portal: Provide an online portal for admissions, allowing prospective students and parents to apply and track their application status.
- Curriculum Showcase: Showcase the school's curriculum for both primary and secondary levels, highlighting key subjects and learning outcomes.
- News and Events Feed: Provide a dynamic news and events feed to keep students, parents, and staff informed about the latest happenings at GoodHope International School.
- Contact and Support Form: Include a contact form and display the support email (hello@goodhope.com) for inquiries and support requests.
- Image Gallery: Display images related to school events and school culture, making use of generative AI to organize and caption the images

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#003366), symbolizing trust, knowledge, and stability.
- Background color: Light gray (#F5F5F5), offering a clean and neutral backdrop.
- Accent color: Vibrant yellow (#FFC107), used sparingly to highlight important elements and calls to action.
- Body and headline font: 'Nunito', a well-balanced sans-serif, ensures readability and a friendly appearance.
- Use simple, clear icons to represent different sections and functionalities, ensuring intuitive navigation.
- Employ a clean, responsive layout that adapts seamlessly to different screen sizes, ensuring a consistent user experience across devices.
- Incorporate subtle animations to enhance user interactions, such as smooth transitions and hover effects, to create a polished feel.
